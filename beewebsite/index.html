Introduction

Bee Savers is a non-profit organization trying to reduce the deaths of bees and keep their population flying.
There has been a rapid global decline of bees due to many industriual and climate reasons. We are here to spread awareness about
the loss of these very beautiful and useful creatives, and trying to keep them in our world!
